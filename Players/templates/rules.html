Rules

This event is based on BIDDING on players in Titiksha 2k16. This event will have two set of contestants –

• Players
• Bidders

Any contestant can register himself/ herself as player , bidder or both. PLAYERS- Those who’ll register themselves as players will have an initial value of 70 points. BIDDERS- Those registered as bidders will bid on these players (maximum 3 at a time). A bidder who will register as player also can not bid upon himself/ herself. He can bid upon other 3 players.On registeration, every bidder will be given 200 points to start bidding. The initial value of 70 points of bidders will change (increase/decrease) according to their participation / performance in titiksha 2k16 events.

RULES

  1. Sign up on our website by contacting the co-ordinators.
  2. Pay sign up fee of Rs. 20 each as a player or bidder for account activation at Internet Lab, Block - D.
  3. Upon payment of registration fee, bidders will be allotted 200 points to start bidding. Bidders can gain more points to bid @Rs. 10 / 100 points.
  4. Register yourself as soon as possible as the bidding will start from Friday 10:00am to Sunday 4:00pm. The results will be declared within an hour or two from the closing of event.
  5. The winners will be given cash prizes. Two separate leader boards (for players as well as bidders) will be maintained for prize distribution.
  6. The players having highest value in terms of points at the end of the event will be declared winners.
  7. The bidders earning highest points in proportion to the amount invested will be declared winners.
  8. Amount of cash prizes will be announced later on the basis of number of registrations received.
